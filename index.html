I'm Taekyung Kim

I'm a senior undergraduate student of DGIST. My major is robotics engineering and minor is computer vision. I am working as a freelancer at a software company. I'm researching human related robotics in my school, and working on things that I find interesting. Let's start scrolling and learn more about me.

About Me

I was born in 1997, Daejeon, Republic of Korea. I wanted to be a robotics engineer since I was young. I spent 4 years studying abroad in China that make me be good at Chinese. Afer I came back to Korea, I participated in many robot competitions and won many awards when I was in high school. I entered DGIST in 2016 and became a member of ROND (Research Officers for National Defense), which is benchmarked 'Talpiot' of Israel, only elected 25 students by the Government every year. I studied autonomous vehicle in 2018 and our research team became the first prize winner among the UGRP (Undergraduate Group Research Program) groups. I am working in a field of computer vision as a software freelancer since 2018.
