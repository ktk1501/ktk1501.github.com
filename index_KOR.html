I'm Taekyung Kim

I'm a senior undergraduate student of DGIST. I major in robotics engineering and have a minor in computer vision. In DGIST, I conduct research on human related robotics. I work at a software company as a freelancer currently. I work on anything that draws my interest. Contact freely by e-mail.

About Me

I was born in 1997, Daejeon, Republic of Korea. I wanted to be a robotics engineer since I was young. I spent 4 years studying in China and that made me fluent at Chinese. After I came back to Korea, I participated in many robotics competitions and won many awards(Check the AWARDS category). I entered DGIST in 2016. I became a member of ROND (Research Officers for National Defense) in 2017. ROND benchmarked 'Talpiot' of Israel, and every year only elected 25 students by the Government can be a member of ROND. In 2018, I studied autonomous vehicle and my research team became the first prize winner among the UGRP (Undergraduate Group Research Program) groups. In 2019, I was selected for 'Talent Award of Korea'. I do work in a field of computer vision as a software freelancer since 2018.
